Data Backup Details

Back up your data to get rewards (one-time only)
from player missions! Read on for details!



By backing up your game data, you can recover your game data if your data becomes corrupt or if you lose or change your device.

Be sure to back up your data before you change or update your device!

For details on data backup and restoration, from the Home screen, tap "Options" (gear icon), then tap "Data Backup," then tap "Details."



■ How to Back Up Your Data

1. From the Home screen, tap "Options" (gear icon), then tap "Data Backup."

2. Tap the "Link" or "Open" button depending on which backup service you wish to use, then follow the on-screen instructions to link your game account to the desired service.

* Please note that backing up your game data through SQEX BRIDGE does not allow payments to be made using Crysta.

3. Upon completion, your game account will be backed up.

■ Notes
If transfer of data across platforms is required, or game account is lost, please submit a request to the Support Center: sqex.to/dffoo_accountrecovery

Please note that while our Support Center may be able to assist with manual account recoveries, we cannot guarantee that all requests can be fulfilled.
