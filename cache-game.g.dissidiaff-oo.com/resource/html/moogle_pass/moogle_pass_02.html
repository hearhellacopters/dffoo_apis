In addition to gems that come with the purchase of a Moogle Pass Premium, you will also receive the following effects and items:
・Increased point bonus for 30 days from purchase
・Guaranteed red passives from high eidoja
・Additional premium mission rewards
・Moogle tokens
・EX power tokens
・Skip-quest tickets

The count will be calculated by the in-game date, with one day passing at 08:00 UTC.

*The effect begins the moment of purchase, and lasts for 30 days from the following 08:00 UTC.

E.g.:
If you purchase the pass on October 30 at 02:00 UTC, the count starts from October 30 at 08:00 UTC, and it will remain effective until November 29 at 07:59 UTC.

Increased Point Bonus


During Effect period
Summon points, wild points, illusion points, spiritus points, and single-challenge points will receive a bonus of 150% (points × 2.5).
Additionally, enhancement points will receive a bonus of 300% (points × 4.0).
The enhancement point limit for each event will also be increased by 500%.
(You can obtain 30,000 points during a single event.)

*The increased point limit will still apply for a Moogle Pass purchased after an event begins.
If your Moogle Pass expires while an event is ongoing, the increased point limit will remain in effect for the duration of the event.

Additional Premium Mission Rewards

During the active period of a Premium Moogle Pass, you'll be able to collect premium rewards by completing certain missions.

If your Premium Moogle Pass expires before completing a mission, you will still be able to collect the premium rewards for missions started during the Premium Moogle Pass's active period.

For missions completed before purchasing a Premium Moogle Pass, premuim rewards can still be collected if a Premium Moogle Pass is purchased before the mission period ends.
