Resistance to Debuffs


The ability to enfeeble, or "debuff," a target can deviate from the norm in three ways: low, high, and immune.

A debuff to which the target has a "low" resistance will take effect more often than usual.

Conversely, one where the resistance is "high" has a lower chance of taking effect.

If the target is "immune" to a debuff, that debuff will never enfeeble the target.
