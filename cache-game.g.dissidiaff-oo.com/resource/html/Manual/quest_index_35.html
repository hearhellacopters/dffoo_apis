Increased Point Bonus


You can gain additional enhancement points, summon points, wild points, illusion points, spiritus points, and single-challenge points depending on your increased point bonus.

The increased point bonus is the sum of the following two bonuses.
  • Rank Bonus Always applied with the effect increasing based on player rank (+1% for every 20 ranks)

  • Moogle Pass Bonus Effects are as follows for the different pass types:

    • Moogle Pass Additional 150% bonus to enhancement points, summon points, wild points, illusion points, spiritus points, and single-challenge points.

    • Premium Moogle Pass Additional 150% bonus to summon points, wild points, illusion points, spiritus points, and single-challenge points. Additional 300% bonus to enhancement points.

E.g.:
  • A player of rank 200 receives a rank bonus of +10% (Points gained × 1.1)
  • A player of rank 600 receives a rank bonus of +30% (Points gained × 1.3)
  • A player of rank 600 with a Moogle Pass receives a point bonus of 180% (Points gained × 2.8)

    • *The increased point bonus stacks with summon point and wild point bonuses from support items, double EXP, and special boosts.
    *The increased point bonus also stacks with the illusion point bonus from World of Illusions Divine boosts.
    *Moogle Pass and Premium Moogle Pass are paid items valid for 30 days.
    You can purchase them from the Home screen or the Increased Point Bonus screen.
    To learn more about each item, tap Details on the Purchase Moogle Pass screen.
Tap on Rank to see your active increased point bonus.
You can also view your increased point bonus from the Results screen.
