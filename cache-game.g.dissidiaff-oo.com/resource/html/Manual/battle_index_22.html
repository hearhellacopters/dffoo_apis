Enemy Recast Abilities


How the Recast Gauge Fills

• Each turn of an enemy with a recast ability will slightly fill its gauge. How the gauge fills may vary by enemy.
　
• The gauge of an enemy with a recast ability fills more when that enemy inflicts a Break.
　
• Some enemies may have a full gauge at the start of a wave of battle, or change how easily their gauges fill during battle.
