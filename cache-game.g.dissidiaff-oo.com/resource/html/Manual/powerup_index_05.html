Selling Equipment




Items Obtained with Sale

The items obtained via sale varies depending on the gear sold.

  • 5★ Weapons 1 power stone, plus 1 additional power stone for each limit break level (up to 4 power stones in total). *This does not apply to 5★ weapons obtained by enhancing rarity.

  • Unique FR Weapons 1 high power stone. If limit breaks have been performed on the weapon, an additional high power stone is awarded for each limit break (up to 4 high power stones).

  • Unique BT Weapons 5 burst power tokens. Limit breaks cannot be performed on burst weapons, and therefore the number of tokens that may be obtained from one sale cannot be increased.

  • You can obtain materials needed for refining by selling weapons and armor.

    • For more information on materials that can be obtained:

    Realization


  • Realized BT weapons cannot be sold.
